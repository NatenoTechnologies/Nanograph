Presenting the Nanograph

What is it?

It is a contact lense that will allow you to take photos with a quality of 108 Megapixels

You can take photos or videos by making some specific movement

All the photos and videos you take will be stored in the NanoCloud, It has a maximum storage of 32GB but you can upgrade your plan for making this up to 1TB!

It works by a chip injected in you nervous system, that lets you to make zoom, make slowmotion videos and more things!
